# Aerodynamics

## The Core Equation
Solar racing is a game of energy margins. The only input is the sun (4 m² array). 
The only job is to waste as little of it as possible.

**Maximise solar input → Minimise drag loss**

## Why Aerodynamics Dominates
Aerodynamic drag scales with the **square of velocity**. Beyond a certain speed it completely 
overtakes rolling resistance. Our model confirmed aerodynamics as the single most consequential 
design domain on the car.

## Design Philosophy
Three drag components targeted: pressure drag, viscous (skin-friction) drag, and interference drag.

Strategy:
- Long forward canopy houses the 4 m² solar array
- Establishes a clean, predictable boundary layer over the main body
- Driver pod and wheel fairings shaped to perform within this pre-conditioned flow

## CFD Results

| Vehicle | Cd |
|---|---|
| Arka | **0.13** |
| Tokai Challenger (world reference) | 0.11 |

Achieved within BSVC constraints (maximise solar panel area, front-facing panel layout) 
which restricted pure aerodynamic optimisation compared to unconstrained monohull designs.

**35% reduction in Cd** achieved through iterative CFD validation across design cycles.
